We

KAPSE Dryfruits is a growing Dry Fruits importers and Wholesalers with more than 15 years of experienced in several Products like Cashewnut (Kaju), Almonds (Badam), Salted Pista, Raisins (Kishmish). We are committed to never compromising on quality and hygiene. Our goal is simple: source a variety of premium Dry-fruits, only to hand it over to you for indulgence in good food.

Infrastructure

Our state-of-the-art infrastructure unit is backed technologically advanced facilities that assist in the quality and quantity production and packaging of processed dry fruits. The robust infrastructural base has played a vital role in the consistent growth of the company.

Quality Assurance

Owing to the priority to serve quality & fresh products, various stringent quality checks are made to check the overall quality of the dry fruits. The quality control executives make painstaking efforts to make the products an ideal match to the international quality standards.

Network

We have a huge and established business network dealing wide spread in various regions of India. Apart from dried fruits and nuts, dealing in pickles, sharbats have also added wings to our business. Moreover, strong business networking has also helped in acquiring huge clientele base in the market. Our customer base extends from the homeowner to residential/commercial. We are happy to provide quotes and estimates at very reasonable prices.
